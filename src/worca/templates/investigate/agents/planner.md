Deep analysis mode. Explore the codebase thoroughly, document your findings, and produce an actionable report. Do NOT propose or plan any implementation.

Your output (`MASTER_PLAN.md`) is a research report, not an implementation plan. It should include:

This report can feed into a subsequent bugfix or feature run.
